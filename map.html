Seeking Truth

Spiritual Guidance

Understanding the heart and atmosphere of our assembly.

What is the atmosphere of your worship?

Our services are characterized by the **tangible presence of God**. We believe in spirited, heartfelt worship and the prophetic flow of the Holy Spirit. Expect an atmosphere of faith where the Word is preached with power and miracles are a natural occurrence.

Can I receive personal prayer or ministration?

Absolutely. At the end of every service, we have an **Altar Call** and a time for personal ministration. Our ministers and prayer team are always available to stand in agreement with you for healing, deliverance, or spiritual breakthrough.

How do I grow deeper in my walk with Christ here?

Beyond our Sunday services, we encourage every member to join our **Mid-week Bible Study** and our prayer wings. We are a "teaching" ministry, focused on equipping you with the spiritual tools needed to become a "fisher of men" in your daily life.

Is the Holy Spirit welcome in your gatherings?

The Holy Spirit is the **Senior Administrator** of our assembly. We do not quench the Spirit; we provide room for the gifts of the Spirit to operate decently and in order, ensuring that every soul is edified and God is glorified.
